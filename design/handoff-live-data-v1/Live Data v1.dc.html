FIRST-TIME SETUP
1Plug the adapter into the OBD2 port — driver footwell, above the pedals.
2Turn the ignition on. The engine can stay off.
3Tap Scan above.
4Pick your adapter — usually "VEEPEAK".
5Done. It reconnects by itself every drive after this.
Don't pair in Bluetooth Settings. The adapter accepts one connection at a time — pairing there blocks the app from finding it.
NOT FINDING IT?
· Adapter LED on? Reseat the plug.
· Ignition on?
· Another OBD app connected? Force-quit it.
· Unplug, wait 10 s, plug back in, scan again.
